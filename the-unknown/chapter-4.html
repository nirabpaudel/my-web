Chapter Four

The next day, I wanted to speak with Autumn to get answers to some of my concerns. So, I went to her house. But when I got there, I was shocked to hear that Autumn had already moved out of the house and shifted to a different place. The new neighbors were already moving their items into the house. Just then, I got introduced to a familiar face. It was Steven. He was an old friend of mine whom I met in middle school. He used to hang out with Autumn and me during lunch break, and we always had a good time. "Hi, I didn't realize you would be my neighbor," he said, shaking my hand.

Although I had promised Autumn not to tell anyone about the picture, I couldn't help but tell Steven. I informed him everything about it, from the poster to seeing the shadow in their new home. But he thought I was being ridiculous and asked me to chill out. He wasn't ready to believe anything I said, so I just let it be and returned to my home.

That night, I felt like someone was walking outside my house. So, I slowly peeked out of the window. To my surprise, there was nobody there. I thought I was just tired and went to sleep.
